Welcome to the game

The Rules: Your goal is to gain a 100 treasure. treasure is your saved Total points. To gain Total points you click the throw dice button but beware if you roll a 1 you lose all your Totalpoints and you must press Retry.
Finally to save your Total Points you click the Freeze Dice button and then your Total points are added to your treasure.
